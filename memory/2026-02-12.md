### SaaS Resource Framework - How to Use Each Platform

**Indie Hackers (Real-time Community)**
- Best for: Raw, unfiltered founder journeys, real-time tactics
- Tactic: Filter by "Bootstrapped" + "SaaS", sort by revenue
- Study "Milestones" section - founders post exact tactics used
- Ask tactical questions, not generic ones ("What channel got you to $5K MRR?" not "How do I grow?")

**Starter Story (Curated Library)**
- Best for: Structured case studies, benchmarking
- Each interview follows consistent Q&A pattern for easy comparison
- Focus on: startup costs, MRR/ARR, team size, marketing channels
- "How You Grew" section = most valuable for tactics

**MicroConf Video Vault**
- Best for: Deep frameworks, polished tactical presentations
- Not motivational - actual tactical breakdowns
- 170+ hours of free content from proven founders
