## Indie Hackers Content That Converts

### Content Types Ranked
| Type | Engagement | Traffic |
|------|------------|---------|
| Journey/milestone with $ | Highest | High |
| Post-mortems with lessons | High | Medium-High |
| Tactical playbooks | High | Medium-High |
| Technical deep-dives | Medium | Medium |
| AMA/Q&A | Medium | Low |
| Direct product announcements | **Lowest** | Low |

### Headline Formula That Works
**Format:** [Specific Number] + [Transformation] + [Timeframe]

Examples:
- "$0 → 1,200 visitors in 6 weeks"
- "How I grew from 0 to $10K MRR in 5 months"
- "$413/month after 3 failed products"

### What Fails
- "Check out my new SaaS"
- "I built a tool for X"
- Generic success claims without numbers

### Post Structure
1. Hook with personal struggle and failed attempts
2. Pivot point ("Then I discovered…")
3. Numbered tactics with specific results
4. Honest admission of what failed
5. Call-to-action as question to community

**Length:** 750-1,500 words for tactical posts

## Waitlist Strategy Fundamentals

### Robinhood Example
- 1 million+ users on waitlist before launch
- Spent essentially nothing on user acquisition
- Used referral mechanics (see position, move up by referring)

### Optimal Waitlist Duration
- **2-4 months** generally ideal
- Too short: don't build sufficient demand
- Too long: people forget why they signed up

### Waitlist KPIs
- Target: 1,000+ highly qualified signups for product validation
- Focus on conversion metrics, not total numbers
- Industry variance: 10-60% conversion from waitlist

### The Email > Sale Truth
Product Hunt is discovery channel. Optimize for **email capture**, not immediate sales.
- Target: 1,000+ emails
- Clear CTA for email capture
- Email campaign must be in place before launch

## 7 Organic Growth Tactics for 2025

### 1. Intent-First Keyword Planning
Focus on precise intent queries:
- "CRM for micro agencies"
- "Freelancer time tracking that auto-invoices"
- "Accounting software for SaaS consultants"

### 2. Search-Friendly Product Tutorials
"How to generate proposals inside [your product]" → attracts users searching for solutions

### 3. Founder-Led Content Cycles
Weekly cycle:
- Mon: Founder's story post
- Wed: Industry breakdown
- Fri: Problem-solving thread or micro case study

### 4. Lightweight Technical Audit Habits
Monthly check-ups: Page indexing, internal links, site performance, canonical issues

### 5. Soft Gated Content
Instead of strong lead magnets, offer ungated content with optional upgrades:
- "Take the extended version via email"
- "Get the full template pack here"

### 6. Small-Scale Partnerships
- Co-created templates
- Shared how-to guides
- Guest sections in newsletters

### 7. Community-First SEO
Test post ideas in Reddit threads, Slack groups, forums before turning into long-form content.

## 90-Day Launch Framework

### Pre-Launch (6-8 weeks out)
- Validate product-market fit through ICP research, discovery interviews
- Build waitlist with teaser campaigns, landing pages
- Create early community (Slack, Discord, LinkedIn group)
- Prepare sales enablement materials

### Launch Day
- Have email campaign ready
- Respond to every comment/question
- Special offer for launch community

### Post-Launch (90 days)
**Metrics that matter:**
- Trial-to-paid conversion rates
- Activation and adoption rates
- Early churn percentage
- **NOT:** vanity metrics like signups without conversions

### The First 90 Days Define Success
- Customers forming impressions
- Competitors watching
- Internal team under pressure
- Goal: Turn early users into loyal advocates by day 90
